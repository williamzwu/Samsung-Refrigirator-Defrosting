Steps to defrost Samsung Refrigerator (Side-by side model, Right Side)

  • Step 1. Take out the bottom drawers and shelf.
  • Step 2. Unscrew 2 bottom screws.
  • Step 3. Unscrew 2 middle screws.
  • Step 4. Remove top circuit cover.
  • Step 5. Remove 2 top screws behind the circuit cover.
  • Step 6. Slowly and gradually flip the cover to the left, with the circuit connectors on the left still connected. It is normally frozen so you need to take a little bit time and patience.
  • Step 7. Remove the circuit connectors by pushing down the top-middle locker.
  • Step 8. Remove 4 fan compartment screws.
  • Step 9. Lift the fan with wires connected.
  • Step 10. Clean the ice in the fan compartment. The clicking noise comes from the fan hitting the ice in the compartment.
  • Step 11. Once ice is cleaned, put back the fan compartment and tighten 4 screws. This is the reversal of step 8.
  • Step 12. With the cover panel open to the left, connect the circuit connectors. This is the reversal of step 7.
  • Step 13. Carefully snap back in the cover. This is the reversal of step 6.
  • Step 14. Put back all screws and balance and tighten all top, middle and bottom screws. This is the reversal of step 5, 3 and 2.
  • Step 15. Put back circuit cover. This is the reversal of step 4.
